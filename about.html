About BMSCE IEEE WIE

IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) is a global network of IEEE members and volunteers dedicated to promoting equality among engineers and scientists. It focuses on inspiring young women around the world and continuing their academic interests in their career in the field of engineering and science. BMSCE IEEE WIE aims at encouraging young minds to pursue research in the field of science and is also dedicated to helping them develop the required skills for the industry. We conduct various workshops, technical talks, and seminars. BMSCE IEEE WIE is a great platform where you can collaborate and network with various established organizations, where you get scholarship opportunities and travel grants. Access to WIE newsletter and the opportunity to be published in IEEE WIE magazine are some of the benefits of this chapter. WIE aims to provide different volunteering opportunities and inculcate leadership qualities.

What we aim at?

WIE aims to provide equal opportunities to women by the cohesive efforts of men and women.
